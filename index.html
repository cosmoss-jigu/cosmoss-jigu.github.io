COSMOSS Lab

COSMOSS (COmputer Systems, Memory, and OS Security) Lab

We enjoy building practical systems with focuses on performance, scalability, security, and reliability. In particular, we are interested in the interactions between layers and revisit the design of computer systems in a holistic manner encompassing hardware, operating system, and applications. Our research contributions have been published in top-tier systems conferences and security conferences, and have made a real-world impacts on critical software, including Linux kernel. If you are interested in building and breaking systems with us, please contact Dr. Changwoo Min with your CV.
